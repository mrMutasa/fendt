About UsService Of Excellence

F AND E NDT (PTY) LTD is a reputable and industry-leading provider of non-destructive testing (NDT) and engineering inspection services. With a steadfast commitment to excellence, we have established ourselves as the go-to choice for clients seeking top-tier solutions in this field. Our success is driven by a team of highly skilled engineers and technicians who possess a deep understanding of NDT principles and practices. This expertise ensures that our clients receive comprehensive and reliable results that meet and exceed their NDT requirements. At F AND E NDT, we leverage a comprehensive array of NDT techniques to provide our clients with the highest quality information. Our reports are known for their accuracy, informativeness, and sufficiency, making them invaluable tools for decision-making and ensuring safety and quality in various industries.
